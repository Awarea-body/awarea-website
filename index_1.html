Why this matters

You're not imagining it.

Most fatigue can't even explain itself.

Unexplained fatigue is 2.7× more common than fatigue with an actual identifiable cause. 20.4% of adults are dealing with fatigue right now.

Source: meta-analysis, Frontiers in Public Health, 2023 — 91 studies, 600,000+ participants

The good news? This is trainable.

How fatigued you feel by the end of the day isn't just about what you did — it's about how much you noticed along the way. Body awareness is one of the measurable factors behind how much fatigue builds up.

Source: predictive modeling study on interoceptive awareness and fatigue, 2023

How it works

Three to five minutes, that's it

Each session is short, guiding your attention to one part of your body at a time. No equipment, no stretching, nowhere to be except wherever you already are.

21-day blocks, one area at a time

Sessions are grouped into 21-day blocks. Each block centres on a single area of the body, with a new session unlocking every day.

Catching tension as it builds

You'll begin to notice it as it happens — a tightened jaw, raised shoulders, a held breath. That's the habit taking hold.

A nudge, not a demand

One daily reminder, at a time that works for you — enough to keep the habit alive, never so much that it feels like one more thing on your list.
